Things beginners get wrong

Small habits that save hours of frustration later.

📱

Always add the viewport meta tag

Without it, mobile browsers fake a 980px desktop view. Your layout will look broken on phones.

📐

Use Grid for layout, Flexbox for rows

Grid is the house. Flexbox is the furniture inside it. They work together, not against each other.

🔠

Use clamp() for font sizes

One line of CSS scales your text smoothly across every screen — no extra media queries needed.

👆

Make tap targets at least 44x44px

Fingers are not mouse cursors. Tiny buttons frustrate mobile users. Always give them enough space.
